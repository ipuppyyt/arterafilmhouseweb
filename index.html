ABOUT US

Artera is a group of some close friends (Dhanush,  Aman Rahi,  Abin Saju  &  Jishnu)  who are good in different fields. Together it was originally, their production house. The idea of forming this group dates back in 2018, but formed in 2019. Initially, the name of this group when started was "Popcorn Media". Later it was changed to "ArtEra Film House" when we decided to take it to the next level. At the beginning, we did sme short videos. Our first technically improvised work was a Rap video song called  "Rap Chekuthan" (by MC Taurx). We also initiated a web series containing two parts.

When started, this was a group of only four members. But now the team is expanding. Now we are doing  small scale production works, music video production, short films, and other works to help new artists.
